Welcome to Castle!

Castle is an Internet of Things application for setting up and configuring household sensors and devices.

To begin, choose 'Sensor Setup'' from the menu (the menu icon up top). The setup your castle by defining:

  1. Define your Keeps. A keep represents an area of your domain. For example, a floor in your house, an external toolshed, or a porch.
  2. Specify the layout of your Keeps Each Keep will need at least one Room for storing devices.
  3. Add Devices to a Keeps Room.

At some point in the future this page will be a dashboard with graphs and charts showing the status of devices.
