Welcome to Castle!

Castle is a Internet of Things application for setting up and configuring household sensors and devices.

To begin, choose 'Sensor Setup'' from the menu (the menu icon up top). The setup your castle by defining:

  1. Keeps: A keep represents an area of your domain. For example, a floor in your house, an external toolshed, or a porch.
  2. Rooms: Each Keep will need at least one Room for storing devices.
  3. Devices: Define each Device in a Room.
